About Our School

Founded in 2005, Peshawar Model School has been serving the community with excellence.

  • Classes: Pre-KG to Class XII
  • Student Strength: 1200+ students
  • Faculty: 80+ experienced teachers
  • Campus: 5 acres of green space
  • Location: Near Central Mall, City Center

Academic Programs

Primary Section (Class I to V)

Focus on foundational skills: English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, Arts, and Physical Education.

Secondary Section (Class VI to X)

Advanced curriculum with laboratory work: Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Mathematics, History, Geography, and Languages.

Senior Secondary (Class XI & XII)

Specialized streams: Science, Commerce, Humanities with career counseling.

Our Key Features

Modern Infrastructure

Well-equipped classrooms, science laboratories, computer lab, library, and sports facilities.

Admissions Information

Admission Process:

  1. Online registration on official website
  2. Entrance examination or assessment test
  3. Personal interview (student + parents)
  4. Document verification
  5. Final admission confirmation

Required Documents:

  • Birth certificate
  • Previous school report card
  • Transfer certificate (if applicable)
  • Address proof
  • Passport-size photographs
